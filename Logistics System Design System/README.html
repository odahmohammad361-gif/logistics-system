The visual language for a Jordan-based shipping platform that moves goods between China, Jordan, and Iraq. Bilingual (EN / AR), dark-only, indigo-default — designed to feel calm under heavy operational load.

v1.0 Dark-only Bilingual EN / AR 8 swappable accents React-ready tokens

01 — Philosophy

Calm under load.

Shipping operators look at this product all day. The system favors deep navy backgrounds, three-tier dim text, and one bright accent — so the screen never shouts back. Status is communicated through tinted pills, not loud chrome. Numbers are always tabular. Arabic is a first-class citizen, not a translation afterthought.

Backgrounds stay constant. Accents swap. Status earns its color. Everything else gets out of the way.

— Operating principle

03 — The five vars you actually edit

Retheming, in five lines.

Every accent theme is just five CSS variables. Override them on [data-accent] and the whole product re-skins — tints, glows, focus rings, brand mark.

--primary#6366F1 · default buttons, brand mark base
--primary-dark#4F46E5 · hover & press states
--primary-light#818CF8 · active text on tinted bg, gradient end-stop
--primary-softrgba(99,102,241,.12) · tinted icon bg, active nav wash
--primary-glowrgba(99,102,241,.35) · brand-mark glow, focus halo
